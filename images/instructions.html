How to play DATE NIGHT

DATE NIGHT is a cheeky text adventure game that tests your ability to handle the often complex and changing social configurations of a date. During the course of the game, you will be presented with a series of scenarios: at the end of each one, you must decide how best to react. At times, a polite and easygoing reaction is suitable; however, an assertive reaction can sometimes lead the way to a win. Your success in the game depends on how well you read your date!

Good luck and make good choices!
